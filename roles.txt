 
AI Engineer
Job Role:AI Engineer / Machine Learning Engineer / Artificial Intelligence Developer
Job Description:
An AI Engineer designs, develops, and deploys artificial intelligence and machine learning solutions to solve complex business problems. They work at the intersection of software engineering, data science, and AI research to build intelligent systems that can learn, adapt, and make decisions.
Key Responsibilities:
Design and develop machine learning models and AI algorithms for various applications including natural language processing, computer vision, predictive analytics, and recommendation systems
Collect, preprocess, and analyze large datasets to train and validate AI models
Implement and optimize deep learning architectures using frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Keras
Deploy AI models into production environments and ensure scalability and performance
Collaborate with data scientists, software engineers, and product teams to integrate AI capabilities into products and services
Monitor model performance, conduct A/B testing, and continuously improve model accuracy
Stay current with latest AI research, techniques, and best practices
Document technical specifications, model architectures, and deployment processes
 
 
Test Architect
Job Role:Test Architect / QA Architect / Quality Engineering Architect
Job Description:
A Test Architect is responsible for defining the overall testing strategy, framework, and standards across software development projects. They provide technical leadership in quality assurance and ensure that testing processes align with business objectives and technical requirements.
Key Responsibilities:
Design and implement comprehensive test strategies, frameworks, and architectures for complex software systems
Define testing standards, methodologies, and best practices across the organization
Select and evaluate test automation tools and frameworks suitable for the project needs
Create test automation frameworks from scratch or enhance existing ones
Provide technical guidance and mentorship to QA teams and test engineers
Collaborate with development architects, product managers, and stakeholders to understand requirements and quality goals
Establish performance, security, and reliability testing approaches
Analyze test results, identify patterns, and recommend improvements to testing processes
